Welcome

A Literary Take on Self Growth

Hello and welcome, I'm Dr Lauren Cooper. I'm here for the sensitive souls — the deep thinkers, the quiet observers, the ones who feel everything a little more. Through literature and reflective practice, I help people discover new ways of understanding themselves and the world around them.

About

The Person Behind the Practice

With a PhD in English Literature and years of experience in education, I'm passionate about facilitating deep and meaningful engagement with the self and literature. My work is rooted in the belief that stories have the power to heal, challenge, and transform.

Based in Bournemouth, I work with individuals and groups both online and in person, offering a space that is warm, inclusive, and gently challenging.

My Approach

Rigorous Literary Study Meets Gentle Inner Work

My signature approach blends rigorous literary analysis with reflective, trauma-informed practice. Every session is infused with empathy, curiosity, and a deep respect for the reader's experience. I believe that engaging with great literature is one of the most powerful tools we have for self-understanding.
